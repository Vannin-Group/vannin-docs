Interoperability standards

Greencube is built to exchange clinical data with the systems a hospital already runs. Interoperability is a core capability, not an add-on.

Supported standards

StandardUse in Greencube
HL7 v2ADT admissions feeds, order messaging, results delivery, and interfaces with legacy hospital systems.
FHIRResource-based exchange for patient records, observations, medications, and appointments; the basis of Greencube's modern integration surface.
SNOMED CTClinical terminology across diagnoses, procedures, and problem lists, supporting coded, analysable records.
DICOMImaging integration with PACS and modality worklists across radiology and diagnostic services.

Multi-standard, multi-market

Greencube runs in 14 countries under different national requirements. The interoperability layer maps between local coding systems, national reporting formats, and international standards, so a deployment in one market can meet local rules without forking the platform.

For interface specifications and conformance details, contact the integration team.
