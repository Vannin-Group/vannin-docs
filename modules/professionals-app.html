Greencube by Vannin Group

Professionals app

The Greencube app for professionals lets doctors work with the full clinical record from a smartphone or tablet, at the bedside or anywhere on the hospital premises, without needing to be at a computer.

Access and worklists

Available on Android and iPhone, the app connects to the centre's environment instantly via a QR code and uses the same login credentials as Greencube, with off-site access possible where permissions and setup allow. Hospitalisation worklists support bed and service transfers and rapid patient filtering by name, record number, or location; emergency worklists show triage status and allow discharge; and outpatient worklists cover nursing schedules, arrival notifications, waiting times, medical queries, and patient calls through monitoring.

The clinical record in hand

FunctionWhat doctors can do
AlarmsPatient alarms are displayed on opening an episode and must be reviewed, with the full alarm list available.
Patient overviewClinical progress logged in the app registers automatically in the hospital system with user traceability; anamnesis and evolution can be recorded by voice.
MedicationReview prescribed medication with risk, condition, and note icons; prescribe from medication protocols; scan medication in the room, with automatic validation of administration.
TreatmentCreate, edit, and delete dietary plans, nursing care (including voice entry), and oxygen therapy.
DiagnosesRecord primary and secondary diagnoses coded in ICD-10, drawing on frequently used diagnoses.
ReferralsReceive notifications addressed to the professional or department, respond by voice, and sign with a biometric signature on the device.
Test mapRequest, modify, and remove tests of all types; view every requested test and its reports and results, including values above and below range.
ImagingIntegration with the PACS viewer for X-ray images, with detailed zoom on mobile and access over mobile data.
Vital signsDaily monitoring with the last three readings per sign, and a graphical view of all data recorded during the stay.

Finding the patient

A record search engine offers multiple filters, recent searches, and instant identification by scanning the barcode on the patient's wristband.

Why it matters

The app is built to improve the quality of clinical records and patient care by capturing information at the point of care, to support staff mobility, and to speed up processes such as referrals, while complying with information security regulations. It benefits doctors, patients, and management alike.
