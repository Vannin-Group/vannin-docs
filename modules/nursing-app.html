Greencube by Vannin Group

Nursing app

The Greencube nursing app brings the nursing record to the patient's bedside on a smartphone or tablet, so care is documented where it happens instead of back at the nursing station. Everything entered in the app updates the hospital system automatically.

Access and worklists

The app runs on Android and iPhone, links to the centre's environment instantly through a QR code, and uses the same credentials as Greencube. It is accessible anywhere in the hospital over the internet, and from outside the centre with the right permissions and configuration. Worklists mirror the desktop: hospitalisation with bed and service transfers, quick patient filters, pre-discharge indicators, and treatment change flags; emergency with triage status and the option to release patients; and outpatients with the nursing schedule, arrival notifications, waiting times, and patient calls through monitoring.

Safety and search

Opening any record surfaces the patient's automatic alarms, which must be reviewed. A search engine with filters, recent patients, and wristband barcode scanning identifies the right patient quickly at the bedside.
