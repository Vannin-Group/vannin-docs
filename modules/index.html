Greencube by Vannin Group

Clinical and operational modules

Greencube is organised as a set of modules that share one patient record. Administrative, clinical, logistics, and financial teams each work in their own module, and information flows between them without re-entry.

Core clinical and administrative modules

  • Admissions: patient registration, appointments, bed management, emergency reception, and authorisations.
  • Medical history: the electronic patient record, from anamnesis and diagnoses to test requests, treatment, and signed discharge reports.
  • Nursing: assessment, care plans, medication administration, vital signs, wound monitoring, and nursing worklists.
  • Surgical: preoperative documents, theatre planning, anaesthesia and surgical reports, recovery, and surgical safety.
  • Diagnostics: worklists and signed reports for imaging, laboratory, pathology, endoscopy, and other tests.
  • Pharmacy: unit-dose dispensing, pharmacist validation, therapeutic exchange, outpatient dispensing, and the hospital drug guide.

Operations and finance

  • Procurement: catalogues, suppliers, purchase orders, storerooms, and patient-level consumption allocation.
  • Billing: automatic pre-invoicing from clinical activity, insurer authorisations, collections, cash registers, and medical fee settlement.

Patient and mobile applications

  • Patient app: mobile appointment booking, self check-in, results, reports, and authorisations for patients.
  • Patient portal: secure web access to records, reports, results, and online appointment booking.
  • Nursing app: bedside assessment, drug administration, vital signs, and consumption capture on mobile devices.
  • Professionals app: mobile worklists, prescribing, diagnoses, test requests, and imaging review for doctors.
  • Patient room: device options for point-of-care access at the bedside, from smartphones to trolleys and TV arms.
Full module documentation, training materials, and configuration guides are provided to customers during implementation. Contact the team.
