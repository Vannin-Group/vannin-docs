Greencube by Vannin Group

Security and compliance

A hospital's record system holds its most sensitive data. Greencube is aligned with HIPAA, GDPR, and DCB standards, and is validated at HIMSS Stage 7, an assessment that includes security and data governance capability.

Access control

Role-based access control mapped to clinical roles and care relationships. Access is granted on need, logged on use, and reviewable by the organisation's information governance team.

Audit

Every access to a patient record is logged: who, what, when, and from where. Audit trails are tamper-evident and exportable for governance review and regulatory inspection.

Data protection

Encryption in transit and at rest, environment separation, and data residency options to meet national requirements. Multi-region deployments keep each customer's data under the jurisdiction its regulator expects.

Clinical safety

Greencube deployments follow documented clinical safety processes covering medication safety, decision support rules, and change control, developed with the clinicians who use the system.
