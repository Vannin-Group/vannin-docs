Greencube integration documentation

Greencube is the enterprise electronic health record owned and developed by Vannin Group: in continuous production since 2012, live across more than 300 clinical sites in 14 countries, and validated at HIMSS Stage 7. This documentation covers how hospital IT teams, integration partners, and national programmes connect systems to Greencube.

Requesting access. Detailed interface specifications, sandbox environments, and API credentials are provided to customers and approved integration partners. Contact the Vannin Group team to begin.

Documentation sections

  • Interoperability standards: HL7 v2, FHIR, SNOMED CT, and DICOM support.
  • Integration patterns: admissions feeds, laboratory and imaging interfaces, pharmacy, and national reporting.
  • Security and compliance: data protection, access control, and audit.
  • API overview: how programmatic access to Greencube is structured.

What Greencube covers

Greencube is a full clinical operating system: electronic health record, orders and results, pharmacy, surgery and theatre management, ICU, nursing care plans, imaging (RIS/PACS integration), laboratory, analytics, and patient and clinician applications. Deployments range from single specialist clinics to ten-hospital university networks and national programmes.
